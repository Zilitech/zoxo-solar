About PM SURYA GHAR

PM SURYA GHAR is the national rooftop-solar platform that connects homeowners with certified installers, helps with online registration and monitoring, and simplifies access to government benefits. Installing rooftop solar reduces electricity bills, increases resilience, and contributes to a cleaner environment.

Key Benefits

  • Lower electricity bills — generate power on your roof and reduce monthly bills.
  • Environment-friendly — cut carbon emissions and rely on renewable energy.
  • Transparent process — register, track, and validate installations online.
  • Verified vendors — choose from MNRE-registered and trusted installers.
  • Support & incentives — access government-driven schemes and local utility facilitation.

Who’s Eligible

  • Residential homeowners, housing societies, and individual flats (subject to local rules).
  • Roof/terrace must be structurally suitable and have adequate sunlight exposure.
  • Subsidy/benefit eligibility may vary by state and utility — check your local scheme on the portal.

How It Works — 5 Simple Steps

  1. Check eligibility & estimate: Use the portal calculator or consult an installer to estimate system size and savings.
  2. Request quotes: Compare quotes, warranties, and after-sales support from certified vendors.
  3. Register: Create an account and upload details along with chosen vendor estimate.
  4. Installation & inspection: Installer completes work; your utility conducts inspection and net-metering setup.
  5. Monitor & claim benefits: Track status on portal and claim central/state incentives.

Documents Typically Required

  • Proof of identity (Aadhaar / Passport / Voter ID)
  • Proof of ownership or NOC from landlord/owner
  • Latest electricity bill
  • Technical quote from MNRE-registered installer
  • Bank details (for subsidy transfers)
