Privacy Policy

At Zoxo Solar, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard data when you use our platform, mobile app, or services related to solar installations, subscriptions, and financing.

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide seamless solar solutions and personalized services. This may include:

  • Personal details: Name, contact number, email, and address
  • Payment and financing information for EMI and subscription services
  • Usage data: Platform interactions, solar calculator inputs, and service requests
  • Device & browser information for improved performance and security
  • Feedback and survey responses to enhance service quality

Usage of Information

Information collected is used to:

  • Process orders, bookings, and solar service subscriptions
  • Provide customer support and installation assistance
  • Calculate ROI, subsidy eligibility, and potential savings using our AI-powered tools
  • Personalize content, offers, and communications based on user preferences
  • Improve platform functionality and user experience

We do not sell or share personal information with unauthorized third parties.

Security of User Data

Zoxo Solar implements robust security measures to protect your data:

  • Data encryption for all transactions and sensitive information
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability testing
  • Access control and authentication for internal teams
  • Secure storage of payment and financing details
  • Immediate action on any detected breach or suspicious activity

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience, remember preferences, analyze platform usage, and display personalized content. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Changes to Privacy Policy
