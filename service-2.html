About Us

Zoxo Solar is a unified digital platform that empowers households, MSMEs, and farmers to adopt clean solar energy through verified vendors, easy financing, and AI-powered solutions. We simplify the solar journey—from product selection to installation and subsidy—making renewable energy affordable, accessible, and reliable for everyone.
